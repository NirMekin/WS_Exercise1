Get Movies and Tv Shows by year and rating, click for example (require 'year' & 'rating' parameters):

../getListYearAndRating/:year/:rating

notes:
1. all movies with rating equal or higher to parameter 'rating' and their year equal to 'year' parameter will be return
2. all Tv Shows with rating equal or higher to parameter 'rating' and their year of broadcast are in the range of 'year' parameter will be return
3. Tv-Shows with 'end_year' = 0 indicates they still broadcast up today
